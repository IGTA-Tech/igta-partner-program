# IGTA Programs and Pricing

## Overview

IGTA offers 5 main programs for O-1 visa services. Partners earn commissions on all programs when they refer clients who enroll.

## Program Details

### 1. O-1 Profile Builder - $800/month
**Duration:** 12 months
**Best for:** Professionals scoring 0-60% on O-1 assessment who need to build their evidence

**What's Included:**
- Self-paced evidence building curriculum
- 36 targeted article publications
- Media coverage placement
- Speaking engagement opportunities
- Community access
- Monthly check-ins

**Target Client:** Early-career tech professionals, those without strong public recognition

---

### 2. O-1 Profile Builder Elite - $1,400/month
**Duration:** 12 months
**Best for:** Professionals who want guided support in building their O-1 case

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Profile Builder
- Dedicated case manager
- Weekly strategy calls
- Personalized evidence roadmap
- Priority placement opportunities
- Enhanced media coverage

**Target Client:** Mid-career professionals wanting hands-on guidance

---

### 3. O-1 Filing Accelerator - $2,000/month
**Duration:** 12 months
**Best for:** Serious candidates committed to both building AND filing

**What's Included:**
- Complete Profile Builder Elite services
- 2 full O-1 petition filings included
- Attorney coordination
- RFE response handling
- Premium processing guidance

**Target Client:** Those ready to invest in a complete solution

---

### 4. O-1 Ready Filing - $11,000 - $15,000 (one-time)
**Duration:** 30-60 days
**Best for:** Already-qualified candidates (85%+ assessment score)

**What's Included:**
- Full petition preparation
- Attorney filing
- USCIS submission
- RFE handling if needed
- Post-approval guidance

**Target Client:** Senior professionals with strong existing evidence (publications, awards, high salary, etc.)

---

### 5. Petitioner Services Only - $2,000 - $10,000
**Duration:** Varies
**Best for:** Clients who already have their own attorney but need agent/petitioner services

**What's Included:**
- Agent petitioner arrangement
- Deal memos from partner companies
- Work authorization documentation
- Multi-employer setup

**Target Client:** Those with existing legal representation who need petitioner flexibility

---

## Client Qualification

IGTA uses an AI-powered assessment at aivisaevaluation.com to score candidates against the 8 O-1 criteria:

- **85%+ Score:** Eligible for Ready Filing
- **60-84% Score:** Recommended for Filing Accelerator
- **35-59% Score:** Recommended for Profile Builder Elite
- **0-34% Score:** Recommended for Profile Builder

## Government Filing Fees (Separate)

Clients also pay government fees directly:
- I-129 Petition: $460
- Asylum Program Fee (ACWIA):
  - 25 or fewer employees: $600
  - 26+ employees: $1,500
- I-907 Premium Processing: $2,805 (optional, 15 business days)

## Commission Information

Specific commission rates are shared with approved partners during onboarding. Partners earn:
- Commission on initial enrollment
- Potential recurring commissions on monthly programs
- Bonuses for high-volume referrals (discussed case-by-case)
